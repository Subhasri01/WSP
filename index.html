ABOUT US

Welcome to WESTERN SEEKH PARATHA, Where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Established with a passion for serving delicious BBQ & more tasty dishes in a cozy atmosphere, we strive to provide an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.
